Who We Are

AeroScout is an aviation job search platform dedicated to connecting pilots, cabin crew, and flight attendants with airlines around the world. We aggregate pilot jobs and cabin crew vacancies from airline career pages and job boards, making it easier to find your next aviation career opportunity.

Whether you're a first officer looking for captain upgrade opportunities, a commercial pilot seeking airline pilot jobs, or a flight attendant searching for cabin crew positions, AeroScout helps you find aviation recruitment opportunities and airline careers.

For Pilots

Whether you hold an ATPL, CPL, or are working towards your type rating, AeroScout helps you find the perfect pilot job. We list opportunities for narrowbody and widebody aircraft, short-haul and long-haul operations, low-cost carriers and legacy airlines.

From turboprop pilot jobs to jet pilot positions, regional airline careers to international airline opportunities, our pilot job board covers the full spectrum of commercial aviation careers.

For Cabin Crew

AeroScout is the ultimate destination for cabin crew job seekers. Find flight attendant jobs with airlines that match your career goals. We feature positions requiring various experience levels, language skills, and base locations across the globe.

Start your cabin crew career or advance to senior cabin crew and purser positions. Our listings include detailed requirements for height, reach, languages, and experience to help you find the perfect match.
